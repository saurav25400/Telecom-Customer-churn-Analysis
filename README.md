# 📊 Telecom Customer Churn Analysis | PYTHON,SQL,EXCEL & POWERBI

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by telecommunication companies, as retaining existing customers is significantly more cost-effective than acquiring new ones. This project analyzes historical telecom customer data to identify the key factors influencing customer churn and develops an interactive dashboard to support data-driven business decisions.

The project combines **Python, SQL, and Power BI** to perform end-to-end data analysis, uncover customer behavior patterns, and generate actionable business recommendations aimed at improving customer retention and profitability.

# 🎯 Business Problem

A telecommunications company is experiencing an increasing customer churn rate, leading to revenue loss and higher customer acquisition costs. Although the company collects extensive customer information—including demographics, subscription details, contract types, billing preferences, payment methods, tenure, service usage, and customer risk levels—it lacks clear visibility into the primary drivers of customer attrition.

The objective of this project is to leverage historical customer data to:

* Identify the major factors contributing to customer churn.
* Understand customer behavior across different customer segments.
* Evaluate the impact of contract types, pricing, payment methods, and services on churn.
* Identify high-risk customer groups.
* Build an interactive dashboard to support strategic business decisions and improve customer retention.

# 📈 Key Insights

* Customers with **Month-to-Month contracts** exhibit significantly higher churn than customers on long-term contracts.
* **Electronic Check** users have the highest customer churn rate compared with other payment methods.
* Customers with **higher monthly charges** are more likely to discontinue services.
* **Fiber Optic** subscribers experience relatively higher churn than DSL customers.
* Customers without **Online Security** and **Tech Support** are more likely to churn.
* **New customers** are considerably more likely to churn than existing customers.
* Customers classified as **High Risk** contribute disproportionately to overall churn.
* Customers with longer tenure demonstrate stronger loyalty and lower churn.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

* Encourage customers to migrate from Month-to-Month contracts to long-term plans through loyalty incentives.
* Promote automatic payment methods such as Credit Card and Bank Transfer to improve customer retention.
* Introduce personalized retention campaigns for High Risk customers before contract renewal.
* Bundle Online Security, Tech Support, and Device Protection with internet plans to increase customer value.
* Improve onboarding experiences for new customers to reduce early-stage churn.
* Review pricing strategies for customers with high monthly charges while maintaining service value.
